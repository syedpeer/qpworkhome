DEFINITION

ERPWikipedia

Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) is the integrated management of core business processes, often in real-time and mediated by software and technology.

ERP is referred to as a category of business-management software - a suite of integrated applications that an organization can use to collect, store, manage and interpret data from these many business activities.

ERP provides an integrated and continuously updated view of core business processes.

DEFINITION

ERPWhy use it ?

The Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) System is the electronic System of Record for the enterprise.

The ERP system encapsulates industry Best Practices from hundreds of companies across the industry and vertical for specilizations. It future proofs the organization with the latest updates and industry specifications.

ERP does all this with an integrated solution.
